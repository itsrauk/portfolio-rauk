about me

between figma and the browser

i'm raul. 22 years old, from ribeirão preto. i've been working professionally since 2020 — started at a local agency, then spent time at growbo, a us-based digital marketing agency, doing web design and visual production for their clients. that's where most of my international work came from.

i work in the overlap between design and development. i design in figma, build in wordpress or ghl, and write javascript when nothing off-the-shelf does the job. i care more about whether the result works for the client than about using whatever stack is trending.

what i do

services

web design

sites and landing pages built to work outside of figma, not just inside it. i choose wordpress, elementor or ghl based on what the client can actually maintain after i'm done. i avoid setups that fall apart the moment someone else touches them.

ui/ux design

interface design in figma focused on reducing friction, not adding complexity. i don't optimize for visual density. i optimize for the user doing what they came to do in as few steps as possible.

graphic design

visual materials for businesses that need assets that actually get used — ads, ebooks, email templates, brand pieces. i've worked across healthcare, automotive, recruitment and tech. most projects have a tight deadline and a brief that changes at least twice.

video editing

video editing for social media and marketing using davinci resolve and premiere. i focus on pacing and clarity — videos that make their point before the viewer decides to skip.
